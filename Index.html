Primary Cause

Free Food

Free Food for All is an main motive of our HCF committed to addressing food insecurity in tamilnadu. We provide access to tasty and nutritious food to vulnerable individuals and families regardless of race, religion and nationality.

Organ Donate

Organ Donation is also a main motive of our HCF. It’s the gift of an organ to a person with end stage organ disease and who needs a transplant. The donated organ may be from a deceased donor or a living donor. Any person not less than 18 years of age, are elligible.

Who we are

Helpcare Foundation is proud to serve as a foundation for a diverse range of social activities in our collective journey to enable social justice. To this end, we partner with, train and mentor many individuals in climate action, women's empowerment, gender rights, peace, health & nutrition, among other goals. These individuals may independently go on to create thinking and actions that further their work in these areas. Our goal at Helpcare Foundation is to help create a stronger, more just and more equitable India for all.

Founder

In 2023, Mr. M Senthil kumar along with other Volunteers from the Villupuram District and the government came together to seed Helpcare Foundation (HCF). They played a key role in shaping HCF's management structures and program strategy in the initial years. Few years into our journey we remain a tightly-knit team working to develop and evolve civil society in India.
